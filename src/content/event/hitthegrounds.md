# 🏏 CSE Hit the Grounds 2025 — Beyond the Boundary

**Hit the Grounds** is the annual cricket tournament hosted by the **Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), University of Moratuwa** — a high-energy celebration of sportsmanship, teamwork, and university spirit.

Set against the vibrant backdrop of the iconic University of Moratuwa grounds, this tournament is more than just a game. It brings together students, faculty, alumni, and industry professionals in a thrilling battle of bat and ball, forging lasting connections across academia and industry.

## 🏟️ What Made It Special

### 🤝 Championship Clash
University teams faced off against industry teams in a thrilling battle for the title — proving that talent doesn't stop at graduation.

### 🌟 The Grand Arena
Played on the iconic University of Moratuwa grounds, where every match carries pride and tradition.

### 🔗 Connections That Count
Beyond the boundary, the event forged lasting ties between students, leaders, and innovators — bridging academia and industry through the shared love of cricket.
