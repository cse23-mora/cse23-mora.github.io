# 🌐 SLIoT Challenge 2026 — Empowering SDGs through AIoT & Industry 5.0

The **SLIoT Challenge** is the annual IoT competition organized by the **Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Moratuwa**, in collaboration with **SLT-MOBITEL** and the **Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL)**. It is the biggest IoT competition in Sri Lanka, open to school students, university undergraduates, and innovators across the island.

## 🎯 This Year's Theme

> *"Empowering SDGs through AIoT and Industry 5.0"*

Participants were challenged to develop innovative IoT solutions evaluated on **creativity, value, impact, and technology**.

## 🏆 Competition Categories

- 🏫 **School Category** — For school students
- 🎓 **University Category** — For undergraduates (teams of 3–5 members from the same university)
- 🌍 **Open Category** — For mixed teams and innovators across institutions

## 🏛️ Organized By
