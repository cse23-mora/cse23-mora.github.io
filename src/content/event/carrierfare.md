# 💼 CSE Career Fair 2026 — Bridging Talent & Opportunity

The **CSE Career Fair 2026** is the annual career fair organized by the **Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Moratuwa**, bringing together final year undergraduate students and top industry companies under one roof.

This year, the event was powered behind the scenes by **CSE Batch 23**, who took ownership of the critical pillars that made it all happen seamlessly.

## 🤝 CSE23's Role

### 🏢 Company Coordination
Our batch reached out to and coordinated with participating companies , communications, and ensuring a smooth experience for industry representatives from arrival to departure.

### 🎓 Student Coordination
From guiding final year students on how to make the most of the fair, to managing queues and schedules, CSE23 ensured every student had a fair shot at connecting with their dream employers.

### 📦 Logistics
Stage setup, booth arrangements, signage, refreshments, and everything in between — our batch handled the ground-level logistics to keep the event running without a hitch.

## 📅 Event Details

- 🗓️ **Date:** January 22, 2026
- 📍 **Venue:** University of Moratuwa
- 🎯 **Target Participants:** Final Year CSE Undergraduates
- 🤝 **Organized by:** Dept. of CSE, University of Moratuwa
- ⚙️ **Facilitated by:** CSE Batch 23

It was a milestone day — not just for the final year students stepping into their professional journeys, but for CSE23 as well, proving that our batch is ready to lead, organize, and deliver. 🚀
