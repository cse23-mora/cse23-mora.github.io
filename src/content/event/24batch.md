# 🎉✨ Welcoming the CSE24 Family!  
### A Colourful Beginning to an Inspiring Journey  

The Department of **Computer Science & Engineering** came alive as we officially welcomed the bright and energetic **CSE Batch ’24** into our ever-growing family. Organized with passion and teamwork by **CSE23**, this inauguration was a perfect blend of **celebration, creativity, and connection**.

From fun-filled activities to mesmerizing performances, the event set the tone for the exciting years ahead for the newest members of our CSE community.

## 🎊 Highlights of the Event  

### 🎮 Fun & Interactive Games  
Icebreakers, team challenges, and creative activities helped CSE24 bond instantly.  
Laughter, teamwork, and new friendships  the perfect start to the journey!

### 🎵 Musical Performances  
Talented performers from CSE23 lit up the stage with energetic music, rhythm, and unforgettable vibes.  
A celebration isn’t complete without music  and this one was truly special.
