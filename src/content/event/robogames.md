# 🤖 IESL RoboGames 2025/26 — Engineering the Future

**IESL RoboGames** is the annual robotics competition organized by the **Department of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Moratuwa**, in collaboration with the **Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL)** and **SLT-MOBITEL** — Sri Lanka's premier platform for the next generation of robotic innovators.

## 🏆 Competition Categories

### 🏫 School Category
Program virtual robots using **Webots simulation software**. A three-phase online competition with in-person finals. No physical robot building required — pure programming and logic!

### 🎓 University Category
Advanced Drone programming using **Webots simulation**. A three-phase competition culminating in in-person finals, focused on algorithmic excellence and strategy for undergraduates from UGC-approved universities.

### ⚔️ Open Category — BattleBots
Design and build **physical combat robots**. A knockout tournament format with head-to-head battles testing engineering skills and strategic planning. Open to all!

## 📅 Key Dates

| **April 26, 2026** | **Grand Finale @ University of Moratuwa 🏆** |

## 🌟 About the Organizers

- **University of Moratuwa** — Department of Computer Science & Engineering
- **IESL** — Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka, the apex engineering body with ~16,000 members
- **SLT-MOBITEL** — Sri Lanka's leading digital service provider

The Grand Finale on **April 26, 2026** at the University of Moratuwa brought together the best teams from all three categories for an action-packed day of innovation and intense BattleBot showdowns. 🔥
