# 🎭✨ Mavisuru Ragasoba — A Day to Remember  
### University of Moratuwa | Faculty of Engineering

The annual **Mavisuru Ragasoba** brought together the entire **University of Moratuwa, Faculty of Engineering**, for a spectacular showcase of talent, creativity, and artistic expression. Students from every department lit up the stage with drama, music, and cultural performances — but one performance stole the spotlight completely.

## 🚢💙 "Titanic 2.0" — By CSE23  
### A Performance That Made Waves

The **CSE23 batch** delivered a hilarious and brilliantly crafted stage play titled **“Titanic 2.0.”**  
With perfect comedic timing, clever storytelling, and stunning stage presence, the drama quickly became the **fan-favorite of the entire night**.

Audience members across faculties called it:

> *“The best drama of the entire event!”*  

From jaw-dropping scenes to unforgettable characters, *Titanic 2.0* showcased the creativity, unity, and talent that makes CSE23 truly special.

## 🌟 Event Overview  

### 🌌 A Celebration Across Faculties  
Mavisuru Ragasoba is more than an art festival — it’s a platform where all departments come together to celebrate culture, creativity, and university spirit.

### 🎤 Musical Acts & Cultural Performances  
Alongside drama, the night featured music, dance, and skits that turned the entire evening into a colorful cultural carnival.

### 🤝 Unity Through Art  
The event strengthened bonds among all engineering batches, fostering collaboration, appreciation, and a deep sense of community.
